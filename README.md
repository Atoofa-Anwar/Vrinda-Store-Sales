# Vrinda Store Sales Analysis (2022)

## Objective
Vrinda store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022 so that the store can understand customers and increase sales in 2022.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard

## Dataset
Sales data of Vrinda Store for the year 2022 including:
- Order details
- Customer gender and age
- State-wise sales
- Sales channels

## Key Questions Answered
- Monthly sales and order trends
- Men vs women purchase comparison
- Top contributing states
- Age group contribution
- Best performing sales channels
- Order status analysis

## Insights
- Women customers contribute around 65% of total sales
- Adult age group (30–49 years) contributes the most
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are top states
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra generate maximum sales

## Final Conclusion
Target women customers aged 30–49 years from Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh by offering discounts and promotions through Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
